About the Project:

GeoRisk is an advanced monitoring platform designed to track and analyze political risks and international relations worldwide. Using real-time data collection from verified news sources and analytical models, the system provides users with comprehensive insights into geopolitical events, socio-economic conditions, and emerging threats.

This tool empowers researchers, policymakers, and analysts to make informed decisions by delivering transparent, reliable, and timely information. The platform's features include fact-checked articles, risk scoring, and dynamic visualizations to better understand complex political landscapes.

Our mission is to enhance global awareness and contribute to peaceful and stable international cooperation through data-driven monitoring.
